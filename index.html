The problem we solve

Slaughterhouse blood is discarded while feed protein remains expensive.

Blood that could be bioconverted into animal feed protein concentrate is often washed away, creating a costly and difficult waste problem.

01 / The waste side

Slaughterhouse blood contains valuable protein.

When it is discarded or poorly handled, slaughterhouses lose a useful raw material and create an environmental problem.

→
02 / The feed side

Feed makers need affordable protein concentrates.

Engukuku supplies a locally produced blood protein concentrate for use in balanced animal feed formulations.

How we bioconvert blood

From slaughterhouse blood to protein concentrate.

We collect fresh slaughterhouse blood, stabilise it, remove moisture, concentrate the protein and prepare it for animal feed formulation.

Stage 01

Collect

Fresh slaughterhouse blood is collected before it is discarded or spoiled.

→
Stage 02

Stabilise

Controlled handling limits spoilage and prepares the blood for bioconversion.

Stage 03

Concentrate

Moisture is removed to produce a stable, protein-dense feed ingredient.

Stage 04

Supply

The protein concentrate is ground, tested, packed and supplied for animal feed formulation.
